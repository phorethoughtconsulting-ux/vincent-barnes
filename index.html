About

Faith, family, and the long game.

I'm Vincent Barnes — a Navy veteran, entrepreneur, husband, and father. I'm building a life and a portfolio of businesses on the same things the Navy taught me: discipline, ownership, and showing up when it's hard.

Today I put that to work building companies, investing in real estate, and mentoring people who want to build something that lasts. My faith and my family come first — everything else is in service of leaving them, and the people around me, better than I found them.

And I share the journey openly — the wins, the lessons, and the systems — so the next person doesn't have to start from zero.
